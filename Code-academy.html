THE HYBRID LEARNING CONCEPT

Learn through immersive hands-on training and put everything you’ve learned into one final project.

Our unique hybrid concept offers a new learning experience that lets you enjoy the comforts of remote study and reap the benefits of learning on campus, all in a single course.

For those who don’t want or can’t travel to our campus in Berlin, we also offer an online-only course. So it’s up to you, but we (and our graduates) highly recommend it.

Put simply: if you join us on campus for 3 weeks, you’ll get the chance to meet all your fellow classmates, work together, and finish your final project without having to worry about cooking, cleaning, or anything else that drains your energy and focus.

The only thing that’s left for you to do is to make your final project the best possible. No distractions. No other commitments. Just 100% pure focus on your future career.
